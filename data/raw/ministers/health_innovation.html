Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Health Innovation and Safety

Organisations: Department of Health and Social Care
Current role holder: Dr Zubir Ahmed MP

Contents

  1. Responsibilities
  2. Current role holder
  3. Announcements

Responsibilities

The Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Health Innovation and Safety is responsible for:

  • NHS data and technology
  • medicines and medtech (medical technology)
  • life sciences, research and innovation
  • international and trade, including devolved governments
  • patient safety
  • blood, transplant and organs
  • disabilities and SEND (special educational needs and disabilities)
  • hospital discharge and clinical pathway reform

Current role holder

Dr Zubir Ahmed MP

Dr Zubir Ahmed MP was appointed Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State at the Department of Health and Social Care on 6 September 2025. He was elected as the MP for Glasgow South West in July 2024.
